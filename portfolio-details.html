"INTAYON PA UNION"

PANAGWAN is a brand design project for a travel agency focused on showcasing the beauty of Pangasinan and La Union. The project aims to create a unique and memorable brand identity that encapsulates the essence of these two beautiful provinces in the Philippines. Through thoughtful design, PANAGWAN seeks to inspire travelers to explore the hidden gems, picturesque beaches, and vibrant communities that make Pangasinan and La Union truly special destinations.
